Massive Storage

With Fancy Fluid Storage you can store massive amounts of fluids in aesthetically pleasing tanks.

Customizability

Build your fluid tanks out of whatever block you want, from cobblestone to diamond blocks!

Compatibility

Compatibility with many major mods is built in out of the box, including BuildCraft and ComputerCraft

How to use

  1. Build a frame out of whatever solid block you desire. (Minimum dimensions: 3x3x3, Maximum dimensions: 32x32x32)
  2. Fill in the sides of the frame with another block of your choice, ex; glass.
  3. Craft a valve by using the following recipe:
  4. Choose a side you want to place your valve, break a single block and place the valve in the hole.
  5. Right click the valve you placed. The valve will turn green to indicate success.
